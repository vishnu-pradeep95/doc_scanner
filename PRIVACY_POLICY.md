# Privacy Policy for PDF Scanner

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Introduction

PDF Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use our document scanning app.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
- **What**: We request access to your device's camera
- **Why**: To capture photos of documents for scanning
- **Storage**: Photos are processed and stored locally on your device only

### Storage Access
- **What**: We access your device's internal storage
- **Why**: To save scanned documents, PDFs, and images
- **Storage**: All files are stored in app-private storage on your device

### Gallery Access
- **What**: We may request access to your photo gallery
- **Why**: To allow you to import existing photos for scanning
- **Storage**: Imported images are copied to app-private storage

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about what we **do not** do:

- ❌ **No Personal Data Collection** - We do not collect names, emails, or any personal information
- ❌ **No Account Required** - The app works entirely offline without any sign-up
- ❌ **No Cloud Upload** - Your documents are never uploaded to any server
- ❌ **No Analytics** - We do not track your usage or behavior
- ❌ **No Advertising** - We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers
- ❌ **No Third-Party Sharing** - Your data is never shared with anyone

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

| Data Type | Location | Encryption |
|-----------|----------|------------|
| Scanned Images | App-private storage | Device encryption |
| Generated PDFs | App-private storage | Device encryption |
| Processed Images | App-private storage | Device encryption |
| App Preferences | SharedPreferences | Device encryption |

**Note**: Files stored in app-private storage are automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party Services

### Google ML Kit (On-Device)
- **Purpose**: OCR (text recognition) and document edge detection
- **Processing**: All ML Kit processing happens ON-DEVICE
- **Data Sent**: None - no data is sent to Google's servers
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google ML Kit Privacy](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms)

### No Other Third-Party Services
We do not integrate any other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|------------|---------|----------|
| `CAMERA` | Take photos of documents | Yes |
| `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` | Import photos from gallery | Optional |
| `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Import files (Android 12 and below) | Optional |

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: All your documents are accessible in the app
- **Export**: Share or export any document at any time
- **Delete**: Delete any document from within the app
- **Uninstall**: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal data at all, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

## Data Security

Your documents are protected by:

1. **App-Private Storage**: Other apps cannot access your scanned documents
2. **Device Encryption**: If your device is encrypted, so are your documents
3. **No Network Access**: The app doesn't require internet to function
4. **No Cloud Storage**: Documents never leave your device unless you share them
